About Paisley Park

Paisley Park is a simple yet versatile bot that allows you to collect stands, show them to others and friends, and fight with them, aswell as assist you in your moderation and utility needs

Paisley Park has many features and unique to come such as:

  • Moderation features
  • 150+ stands and counting
  • Currency System
  • User Informational Cards
  • Image Manipulation
  • And many more useful commands!
  • Server Management [SOON]
  • Server Protection [SOON]

Donating

Donating  comes with a big packet of customization and boosts your stand. We do not give you any exclusive things that others cannot obtain except a custom stand name. When purchased and contacted a developer, you can customize your stand depending on your donation amount, aswell as receiving a donator badge. We greatly appreciate your support!

Donating includes customization such as

  • Music Compatability
  • Customize your stand
  • Access to Testing
  • Exclusive Recognition and Badge
  • And more coming soon...
